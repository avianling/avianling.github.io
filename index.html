LEGACY

I'm now working on my dream game: Legacy. Its an ambitious project which uses a bunch of layered AI and combines them with social simulation to create an intricate, living and evolving world. This complex world will become the foundation for a procedural narrative system, which will allow legacy to offer players significantly more freedom than they've ever had before. We're talking orders of magnitude more here. Every action they take can be fully realized. The best way to think of it is to imagine playing Skyrim, where the computer is running the Sims, Age of Empires, and Civilization, behind the scenes. This allows relationships to change, empires to fall, towns to change and move and grow. These simulations form the cornerstone of a more relaxed, freeform storytelling system which will create the kind of fantastical stories players expect from an RPG.

We already have a framework in place, which handles the world, content streaming and serialization. Art is on the way, and contacts are in place to provide audio and art support. Creating of the simulation backbone is underway, and extensive research has been completed to determine how exactly it should be structured. It's incredibly exciting to be working on this project, knowing what it has the potential to become.
