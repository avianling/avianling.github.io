I AM A PROGRAMMER

FEATURED PROJECT

I have been programming since I was 12. I studied software engineering at the University of Auckland, graduating in 2014, and I work at Ninja Kiwi developing Unity games. I like to experiment with new ideas by creating prototypes. I am interested in procedural generation, open worlds, streaming and circular coordinate systems.

While building Fortress: Destroyer, I rapidly created the initial prototype, then played a major role in the development team. My work on Fortress included a well performing, highly destructable terrain system, UI framework and highly flexible combat backend.

I AM A DESIGNER

FEATURED PROJECT

I design and create games. I push my boundaries by creating different experiences with different techniques. I study existing games, and participate in community discussions on game design. I am particularly interested in creating emergent narrative and plot, and in understanding how game elements drive human emotion. I improve these skills via experimentation, such as designing abilities and enemies in Fortress: Destroyer, designing puzzles in Rocket Maze and creating the universe in One Man's Sky.

Inspired by No Man's Sky, I created a procedural universe called One Man's Sky. Creating a universe where you simultaneously feel the size of the galaxy and that it is all within reach was a unique design challenge which I solved by creating landmarks, a sense of scale and the feeling of immense acceleration.

I AM AN ENTREPRENEUR

I am involved in a small startup aiming to push the boundaries of games - we are currently prototyping our first game. We are creating an evolving social simulation / RPG hybrid, which will create emergent plot and deep player choice through the use of AI and elements from RTS games.

Games have provided me with defining moments in my life, with experiences no other medium can ever give you. My goal is to push the boundaries of what is currently possible with video games, and I believe that this game has that potential.

We are currently looking for additional artists and developers.

I AM A LEARNER

FEATURED PROJECT

I continue to grow my skill base through research, experimentation, conferences and online discussion. I have judged the University of Auckland SESA game jam. I attend game jams regularly and I have reviewed games on the reddit r/gamedesign forum. By challenging ideas about game design, I am able to create more interesting games such as Dioshock.

As part of a university project to develop educational games, I created the diabetes education game Dioshock, which I presented at the Australasian health IT conference, HIKM.

I AM A CREATOR

I created the game Boarding Party for a game jam, then improved it by adding new AI, effects and content creation tools.
