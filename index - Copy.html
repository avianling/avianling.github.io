Under Stranger Stars

Legacy is an experimental title focusing on AI and dynamic narrative. Featuring an advanced simulation engine, Legacy is able to create a living world where characters react to the world around them, adapting their goals, actions & personalities to their unique situations.

Legacy includes utility & awareness based AI for characters around the player, but is able to seamlessly swap to simpler for characters at a distance.

The game also includes a number of technically complex features such as dynamic lighting, a day/night cycle, runtime navmesh generation and unity based editors.
